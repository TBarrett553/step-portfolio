Teanna Barrett’s Portfolio

Hello I’m Teanna Barrett and this is my portfolio. I'm creating this portfolio for my second week project as a part of the STEP internship. I not only wanted to share my professional side, I want to personally introduce myself. I have structured this app as a choose your own adventure of sorts. Each panel of the webpage will have some information about me and choices displayed at the bottom. As you continue to pick choices you will be able to learn specific things about me and the work I hope to do as a Computer Scientist. Once you reach the last panel, you will then be directed to a Guest Book of sorts. This will allow you to write a short message and record that you have participated in my little project. Then you will be redirected to the beginning so you can try a new path. Thank you for doing this and have fun!

Future Outlook

My central goal is fairly straightforward. I aim to gain technical skills in Computer Science so the world can be a better place to live for everyone. I keep myself on track with general professional and social impact goals.


Professional/Academic Goals
  1. Earn a Computer Science undergraduate degree (with a minor in Economics)
  2. Earn a Master’s in Data Science
  3. Earn a PhD in Economics with a Concentration in Data Science and Social Justice
  4. Conduct Academic or Professional Research in Social Justice Reform with Data Science

This plan is very tentative. I have no idea how my goals will change over time but this is my ideal plan right now.


Social Impact Goals

There is a lot of inequality in our society today (racial, gender, class, etc) that needs to be amended. Our society has come a long way but I believe that technology can be used to make more comprehensive changes to our world for the better. More specifically, I want to build technology that directly addresses injustice and provides effective solutions. In order to do this, I aim to work thoughtfully, honestly, and to be held accountable.
